In this section you can add movies to a list behind the scenes. You can check your latest entry above. The list disappears on browser reload.

"Filter" button filters your list by title. If your entry is valid, all movies with that title will appear below in a list. Choose the desired movie from the list and press the "Search Info" button. Here, you can also search for a movie's info directly, without adding it in the list in the previous section.
